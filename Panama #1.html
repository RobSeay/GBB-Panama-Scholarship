Understanding the Investment

What Does the Trip Cost?

We want cost to be a conversation, not a barrier. Here's exactly what you're looking at — and what the scholarship covers.

$1,590
Program Fee

Covers in-country transportation, lodging, meals, materials, and access to the Global Brigades consulting framework. All scholarship recipients remain responsible for this fee. Fundraising efforts may reduce this amount, but the student is ultimately responsible for any balance.

~$400–$700
Round-Trip Flight

Flight cost varies by booking window and departure city. GBB books flights directly on your behalf — you do not purchase a ticket yourself. Flight booking is subject to confirmed arrival/departure windows provided by Global Brigades.

Up to $567
Scholarship Coverage

Each of the three scholarships covers the flight cost up to $567 (round-trip). If your flight exceeds this cap, the remaining balance is your responsibility. The scholarship applies to the flight only — not the program fee.

Total out-of-pocket with scholarship: approximately $1,590 program fee + any flight amount above $567. Total without scholarship: approximately $1,590 + full flight cost.
Three Awards Available

Scholarship Opportunities

Each scholarship recognizes a different dimension of what it takes to make a service trip meaningful. Find the one that speaks to your story.

01
Access & Impact

Access & Impact Scholarship

For students who face real financial barriers to participation — and who will bring genuine commitment to the communities we serve.

Who Should Apply

Students for whom the combined cost of the program fee and flight represents a meaningful financial hardship. This award prioritizes demonstrated need alongside motivation.

What It Covers

Round-trip flight, booked by GBB, up to $567.

Selection Criteria
  • Demonstrated financial need or barrier to participation
  • Clear articulation of why this trip is meaningful to you personally
  • Commitment to completing all pre-trip requirements and the $1,590 program fee
  • Alignment with GBB IU's values of service, humility, and learning
02
Consulting Excellence

Consulting Excellence Scholarship

For students who bring strong analytical and consulting skills — and who will deliver exceptional value to our partner businesses in Panama.

Who Should Apply

Students with demonstrated consulting, analytical, or business problem-solving experience — through coursework, internships, case competitions, or student organizations.

What It Covers

Round-trip flight, booked by GBB, up to $567.

Selection Criteria
  • Evidence of consulting or analytical skill (resume, essay, interview)
  • Ability to communicate structured thinking clearly
  • Track record of preparation and professionalism
  • Motivation to apply skills in a resource-constrained, real-world setting
03
Leadership & Ambassador

Leadership & Ambassador Scholarship

For students who will actively recruit, represent, and grow GBB IU — carrying the mission beyond the trip itself.

Who Should Apply

Students with a history of campus leadership, community building, or organizational involvement who commit to serving as an ambassador for GBB IU after returning.

What It Covers

Round-trip flight, booked by GBB, up to $567.

Selection Criteria
  • Demonstrated leadership in clubs, organizations, or community settings
  • Specific vision for how you will represent and promote GBB IU post-trip
  • Ability to inspire and connect with prospective members
  • Enthusiasm for the GBB mission beyond the trip itself

How We Choose

Selection Process

Our goal is a fair, consistent, and thorough review. Every applicant goes through the same steps.

Step 1
Application Review

The selection committee reviews all submitted applications, resumes, and essays. Reviewers assess each application against the scholarship-specific criteria and score independently.

Step 2
Finalist Selection

Top applicants per scholarship are identified as finalists and invited to a Zoom interview. Executive Board members may participate in review to provide broad organizational perspective.

Step 3
Finalist Interviews

Interviews run 10–15 minutes via Zoom. All finalists are asked the same set of questions and evaluated against a standardized scoring rubric — ensuring a fair comparison across candidates.

Step 4
Final Decision & Notification

The selection committee deliberates and awards one recipient per scholarship. All applicants — finalists and non-finalists — are notified of the outcome by the decision date.

Important Policies

Scholarship Policies

Please read these carefully before applying. Accepting a scholarship means agreeing to these terms.

Passport Submission — 48-Hour Deadline

Upon accepting a scholarship, recipients must submit complete passport information — including a clear photo of the passport photo page — within 48 hours. This information is required before GBB can book your flight. Failure to meet this deadline may result in forfeiture of the scholarship, with no exceptions outside of documented emergencies.

Flight Cap & Booking Method

The scholarship covers up to $567 per recipient for a round-trip flight. GBB IU books all flights directly on the recipient's behalf — recipients do not purchase their own ticket. If the actual flight cost exceeds $567, the recipient is responsible for the difference. All bookings are subject to confirmed arrival and departure windows provided by Global Brigades.

Program Fee Responsibility

The scholarship covers the flight only. All recipients remain responsible for the $1,590 program fee, regardless of scholarship status. Fundraising efforts through GBB IU may reduce this amount, but the student is ultimately responsible for any remaining balance.

Withdrawal Policy

If a scholarship recipient withdraws from the trip after GBB has booked their flight, they may be held responsible for any non-recoverable costs incurred. Airline credits, when applicable, typically remain in the traveler's name and are not transferable to GBB IU or other participants. Exceptions to this policy are considered only for documented emergencies at the discretion of GBB IU leadership.
