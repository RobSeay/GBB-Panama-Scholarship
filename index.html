Scholarship Overview

About the Program

Global Business Brigades Panama offers students an immersive experience combining professional development with community service.

Participants collaborate with local entrepreneurs, gain hands-on consulting experience, and explore Panamanian culture during a two-week brigade.

  • Networking with local business leaders
  • Hands-on consulting projects
  • Cultural immersion activities
  • Partial travel cost assistance

Our Foundation

Global Brigades Values

The scholarship and program are guided by Global Brigades' core organizational values.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering local leaders and community members to achieve their own economic and social goals sustainably.

Community Ownership

Programs are built alongside communities — not for them — ensuring local stakeholders lead decision-making and sustain impact long-term.

Holistic Model

We address interconnected health and economic factors together, recognizing that lasting change requires a comprehensive approach.

Collaboration

Students, local volunteers, NGO partners, and communities work side-by-side — building bridges across borders and disciplines.

Policies

Important Requirements & Rules

Scholarship recipients must comply with these policies to maintain funding.

Passport Deadline

Recipients must submit passport information within 48 hours of acceptance. Failure may lead to forfeiture.

Withdrawal Policy

If a student withdraws after flight booking, they are responsible for non-recoverable costs. Airline credits remain in the student's name. Exceptions only for documented emergency.
